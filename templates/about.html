About calculating chess grades

MyChessGrade allows you to calculate your chess grade after every game.

All you need to do is enter in your current grade and your recent game results.

Your grade is calculated from your grade, your opponents grade and the result of the game (win, loss or draw).

Once you've added these just click on the calculate button to see what your new grade is.

Supported Grading systems

There are a number of different grading systems used by chess federations.

The most common grading system is ELO. This is the grading system used by FIDE, the international chess federation.

However, other chess federations, such as the US chess federation, use a different implementation of the ELO grading system.

We currently support the ELO FIDE grading system and the English Chess Federation Grading system.

ELO and other grading systems

Different implementations of ELO are used worldwide to compare the ability of players of many games (not just chess).

The English Chess Federation has a grading system that is not based on ELO.

We support that too.
